FAQ

+

What is the website of an Academy Platform?

An Academy platform is a platform that was designed and developed from scratch to be a first assistant to students, it is comprehensive and includes lectures, books, quizes and its own translator

+

Will it remain without ads?

The purpose of this platform is not profit. But there are other factors that are in the background until this platform appears in front of you. It is possible that the ads are activated to cover the cost of hosting and other things. It won't be annoying.

+

What is an academy platform translator?

Translator academy platform has been developed so you can do everything you want inside the site even translation and there is no need to get out of here and go to translate in another site. Don't worry, it's dependable.

+

How do I report something not working?

I'm sorry to hear that. But do not hesitate to contact me through the "Contact"section located above this part directly.
